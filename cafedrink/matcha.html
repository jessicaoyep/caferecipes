Stagger Double Matcha Latte

story behind this recipe

This recipe reminds me of a cozy afternoon in a small cafe, reading a good book and being transported to another world.

Ingredients: matcha powder, milk, sweetener (optional)

Time to make: 5 minutes

Size: 16oz

instructions:

  1. Whisk 1 tsp matcha powder with 2 tbsp hot water until smooth.
  2. Add 1 cup milk and whisk until combined.
  3. Add sweetener to taste (optional).
