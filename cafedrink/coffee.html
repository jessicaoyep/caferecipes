Wayhome Latte

Ingredients: espresso, milk, vanilla syrup

Time to make: 12 minutes

Size: 16oz

instructions:

  1. Prepare 1 shot of espresso.
  2. Add 1 cup of milk and steam it until frothy.
  3. Pour the espresso over the milk and add vanilla syrup to taste.
